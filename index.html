FAQs

What makes an ergonomic chair different from a regular office chair? −
An ergonomic chair offers adjustable features like adjustable headrest, armrest, lumbar support, and height, designed to promote good posture and comfort. Ergonomic chairs reduce the risk of back pain, neck stiffness, and joint issues by supporting proper posture and spinal alignment. Regular office chairs typically lack these adjustments and support, making them less suitable for prolonged use.
